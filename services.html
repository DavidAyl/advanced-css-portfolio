Services

Services offered by me are all made to order and never made using templates or older designs. We will take the journey together to make sure you are satisfied with the way your site works. I will be in contact with you and giving you updates every step of the way. Each webpage will come with an interactive UI and will adjust accordingly to the pages size.


HTML

Html is a markup language that lays the structure of your page. When you choose me to create your website you get in depth and truly meaningful content. This will be one of the most collaborative parts of the design of your webpage. In this section we will put anything from navigation bars, to hyperlinks and even photos, all of this will get styled later.

CSS

Cascading Style Sheets also known as CSS will be the visual design of your website, this will include colors, spacing and even background images. This will be the page I will focus on the most as it catches the most eyes. I believe the key to a good website is something simple and easy to comprehend. We will choose a color palette, logos, and other stylistic choices to stick to as a foundation.

JavaScript

Javascript is the main language that is going to make your webpage functional and interactive. Through the use of CSS we will be able to create forms to collect clients data, image animations, music and more. Javascript is something that can really make or break your page, if it lacks being interactive, users may be inclined to leaving sooner.
